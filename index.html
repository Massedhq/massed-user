👤 Media Profile Guide

What it is: Your Media Profile is your public page at massed.io/yourusername — your digital business card for collaborators, customers and partners.

Where to find it: Tap Media Profile in the left sidebar.

How to set it up:

1. Upload a professional photo or brand logo.

2. Enter your Display Name exactly as you want it shown publicly.

3. Add your Title or Role so visitors know what you do.

4. Write your Bio — authentic, under 200 words. Tell your story, who you serve, what makes your brand unique.

5. Select your Category to help MASSED match you to the right audience.

6. Add Contact Info — email required, phone and website optional.

7. Click Save Profile. Changes are not auto-saved.

Collaboration tab: Toggle visibility on, pick your genres, browse creators, send and accept requests, message collaborators, and sign NDAs — all within MASSED.

Tips: High-res photo = 3× more views. Update your bio when your focus changes. Pick multiple genres to appear in more searches.

🛍️ My Store Guide

What it is: My Store is your public storefront — it controls which products and services your audience can browse and buy at your MASSED link.

Where to find it: Tap My Store in the left sidebar. Digital Products and Physical Products appear as sub-items underneath.

How to set it up:

1. Toggle Store Live at the top to make your store visible to the public.

2. Use the What's In Your Store toggles to show or hide each product type — Digital Products, Physical Products, Courses, Bookings, Events, Services, Links and Videos.

3. Your Store URL is shown at the top — copy and share it anywhere.

Tip: Only enable the product types you actually have set up so your store looks clean and professional to visitors.

💰 Sales & Payouts Guide

What it is: Sales & Payouts is your financial command center — see all revenue, traffic data, and manage how you get paid.

Where to find it: Tap Sales & Payouts in the left sidebar.

Sales tab: Today's revenue, sessions, conversion funnel, traffic channels with interactive world map, top products, and social media analysis.

Payout tab: Available balance, processing funds, earnings breakdown by source, full transaction feed. Switch to Affiliate Earnings to see referral commissions.

Withdrawing: Connect your Stripe account in Settings → Stripe to enable direct bank payouts.

Graduation: Pay $30/month for 12 months and your plan becomes FREE for 24 months — then just $79.99/year. Track progress in Settings → Billing.

🗺️ How MASSED Works

👤 Media Profile

Your public identity on MASSED — visible at massed.io/yourusername. Set your photo, display name, title/role, bio (under 200 words), category, and contact info. Click Save Profile — changes are not auto-saved.

Collaboration tab: Toggle your visibility on, choose your genres, browse other creators by genre, send and accept connection requests, message active collaborators, and sign legally binding NDAs. Signed NDAs auto-save to the Documents tab — click View to open the full signed document with both digital signatures and timestamps, or Download to save a copy.

My Hearts tab: See everyone who saved or hearted your profile, along with total heart count, hearts this week, and profile view stats.

🛍️ My Store

Your public storefront. Toggle Store Live to make it visible at your MASSED link. Use the product type toggles to control what appears — Digital Products, Physical Products, Courses, Bookings, Events, Services, Links, and Videos. The sidebar has three sub-items: Digital Products, Physical Products, and Courses.

🔗 Web Links

Add any URL to your public page — websites, landing pages, booking links, portfolios, or anything you want your audience to tap. Each link appears as a card on your MASSED profile with a cover image, title, and Open Link button.

📅 Booking

Found in the sidebar under Booking. Add bookable services — each with a name, price, duration, category, description, and optional cover photo. Toggle Booking Page Live to make your booking page visible on your MASSED profile.

Deposit system: When adding a service, toggle Require Deposit on and set a fixed dollar amount or percentage. When a client books, they pay the deposit upfront to secure their date and time. The remaining balance is collected at the time of service.

How clients book: They tap Book Now on your service card, pick a date from the calendar, select a time slot, and confirm. If a deposit is required the button shows the deposit amount — tapping it completes payment and locks in the booking.

📱 Social Links

Connect Instagram, X/Twitter, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, Pinterest, LinkedIn, and Amazon. Each platform has a toggle — flip it on to show that platform publicly on your profile, flip it off to hide it. Only platforms with the toggle on are visible to visitors.

🎬 Video

Upload or link your videos. They appear directly on your public MASSED profile so your audience can watch without leaving your page. Add a title, genre, and duration for each video.

🎟️ Events / Tickets

Create in-person or online events. Set ticket tiers, prices, and capacity. Manage attendees and collect payments through Stripe. Use the built-in Ticket Scanner in the sidebar to scan QR codes at the door on event day. Select Other as event type to type in a custom type.

🔴 Go Live

Click the Go Live button in the top bar. Choose Go Live to connect with your audience with no product, or Go Live & Sell to feature a product at a sale price with an optional countdown timer. Use ⏸ Pause to pause without ending, and End Live to close the session. The live banner stays visible to your audience until you end it.

Sales tab: Today's revenue, sessions, conversion funnel, traffic channels with interactive world map, top products, and social media analysis.

Payout tab: Balance Breakdown (Available Now, Processing, Held), Earnings Overview, and Transaction Feed. Switch to Affiliate Earnings to see Top Earners with Pending/Paid status and Pay Now buttons. Once paid, tap View Receipt to see the receipt — amount, who it was sent to, date and time.

Payout Settings — a sub-item in the sidebar under Sales & Payouts. Configure your payout preferences here.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is MASSED?

MASSED is an all-in-one creator platform that gives you a public profile, a store, event ticketing, live selling, collaboration tools, and analytics — all under your own MASSED link. It is built for creators, service providers, and small business owners who want to sell, grow, and connect in one place.

❓ How much does MASSED cost?

MASSED costs $30/month on the Creator Pro plan. After 12 consecutive months of payments you graduate to a FREE 24-month plan — zero payments, keep every dollar you earn for 2 full years. After that it is just $79.99/year, forever. Track your graduation progress in Settings → Billing.

❓ How do I get paid?

Connect your Stripe account in Settings → Stripe. All sales, ticket revenue, and live selling payments go through Stripe and deposit directly to your bank account. You keep your earnings — MASSED does not take a cut of your sales.

❓ How do I make my store visible?

Go to My Store in the sidebar and toggle Store Live to on. Your store will immediately be visible at massed.io/yourusername. You can also choose which product types appear using the toggles inside My Store.

❓ What is the Collaboration feature?

Collaboration lets you find and connect with other MASSED creators. Go to Media Profile → Collaboration tab, toggle yourself visible, choose your genres, and start browsing. You can send connection requests, message accepted collaborators, and sign a legally binding NDA — all within MASSED. Signed NDAs automatically save to your Documents tab.

❓ How do I go live and sell a product?

Click the Go Live button in the top bar. Choose Go Live & Sell, select a product from your store, set a sale price, choose a timer if you want a countdown, and click Go Live & Sell. Your live banner will appear and your audience can purchase directly during the session.

❓ Can I control what shows on my public profile?

Yes. In Social Links you can toggle each platform on or off — only platforms with the toggle on will appear publicly. In My Store you control which product types are visible. Your store must also have the Store Live toggle on to be publicly accessible.

❓ How do I cancel or change my plan?

Go to Settings → Billing. You can manage your plan or cancel from there. To cancel, click Cancel Plan or contact support directly. We recommend reaching out first — our team may be able to help with any issues before you decide to cancel.

❓ How do I contact support?

Go to Settings → Support and click Send a Message or Create a Support Ticket. Fill in your subject, message, and email. Our team responds within 24–48 hours. For urgent billing issues include your account email in the message.

🌐 Browser Icon Guide

      
    

    

      
What it is: A favicon (Browser Icon) is the small icon that appears in your visitor's browser tab when they open your MASSED page. It makes your page feel like a real website — professional, trusted, and yours.

      
Where to find it: Tap Browser Icon in the left sidebar under the Publish group.

      
How to set it up:

      
1. Click the upload zone and select a PNG, JPG, or SVG file. A square image works best — recommended size is 512×512px.

      
2. Your icon will be automatically resized to 16×16 and 32×32 — the standard favicon sizes for all browsers.

      
3. Watch the live browser tab preview update instantly so you can see exactly how it will look before applying.

      
4. Optionally set a Browser Tab Title — this is the text that appears next to your icon in the tab (e.g. "Ava Stone | Creator").

      
5. Click Apply Browser Icon to My Page to save and publish it to your MASSED link page.

      
Why it matters:

      
✓ Makes your page feel like a real website — not just a link
✓ Builds trust when visitors open your page

      
Tips: Use your logo or a clean version of your brand mark. Avoid text-heavy images — they won't be readable at 16×16px. A simple symbol or initial works best.

🛡️ Account Security

      
    

    

      
Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Go to Settings → Security and toggle on 2FA. This adds a second layer of protection — you'll need a code from your phone every time you log in.

      
Password change: Go to Settings → ⚙️ Settings tab. Enter your phone number and new password, tap Send Code to receive a 6-digit SMS code, enter it, and tap Verify.

      
Login activity: Settings → Security shows your recent login sessions — device type, browser, location, and time. If you see a session you don't recognize, sign out all other sessions immediately.

      
Security tips:

      
🔐 Use a strong password — at least 12 characters with numbers and symbols
📱 Enable 2FA for maximum protection
⚠️ MASSED staff will never ask for your password
🔒 Never share your login credentials

      
For full security settings go to Settings → Security tab.

📅 Booking Guide

      
    

    

      
What it is: Booking lets you add services that clients can book directly from your MASSED page — with date selection, time slots, and deposit collection built in.

      
Where to find it: Tap Booking in the sidebar under the Build section.

      
Adding a service:

      
1. Click + Add Service in the top right.

      
2. Enter the service name, price, duration, and category.

      
3. Add an optional description and cover photo.

      
4. Toggle Require Deposit on if you want clients to pay upfront. Set a fixed dollar amount or a percentage of the total. The remaining balance is collected at time of service.

      
5. Click Add Service to save and publish the card.

      
How the deposit works: When a client taps Book Now, they select a date from the calendar and pick a time slot. If a deposit is required, the confirm button shows the deposit amount (e.g. "Pay Deposit $30 → Book"). Tapping it processes the deposit and locks in the appointment.

      
Managing services: Each service card has a Visible/Hidden toggle to control whether it shows on your profile, and a Delete button to remove it.

      
Booking Page Live toggle: At the top of the Booking screen — flip this on to make your entire booking page visible on your MASSED profile. Flip it off to hide all booking from visitors.
